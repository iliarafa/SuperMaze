Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Lov's Maze ("the App") is developed by Ilias Rafailidis. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All gameplay data (settings, scores) is stored locally on your device using browser storage and is never sent to any server.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Device Permissions

The App may request access to device orientation (tilt controls). This data is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. No personal data is collected from any user regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at iliasr@me.com.
