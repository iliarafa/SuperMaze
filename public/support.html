FAQ

How do I play?
Choose "Race" mode to guide your agent through the maze using swipe or tilt controls. The quantum agent will then solve the same maze using Grover's algorithm. Compare your performance!

What is Observe mode?
In Observe mode, you watch the quantum agent explore the maze. Touch and hold to charge the quantum measurement, then release to collapse the superposition.

Tilt controls aren't working.
Make sure you've granted motion permission when prompted. If tilt feels unresponsive, try the "Calibrate" button to reset the reference angle. Tilt controls require a device with a gyroscope.

The app feels slow or laggy.
Try closing other apps running in the background. The game runs at 60fps and performs best with fewer competing processes.
